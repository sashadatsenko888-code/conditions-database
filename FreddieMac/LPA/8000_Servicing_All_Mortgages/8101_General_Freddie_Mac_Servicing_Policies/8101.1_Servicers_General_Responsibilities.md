Source: Freddie Mac Seller/Servicer Guide  
Section: 8101.1 – Servicer’s General Responsibilities  
Effective Date: 09/10/2025  

---

## Overview
Section 8101.1 outlines the Servicer’s **general responsibilities** when servicing Mortgages owned or guaranteed by Freddie Mac.  
The Servicer must act in a timely, efficient and responsible manner to safeguard Freddie Mac’s interests, maintain adequate servicing facilities, comply with all Purchase Documents, and ensure continuity of operations.

---

## (a) Servicer’s General Responsibilities
The Servicer must protect Freddie Mac’s interests and ensure:

- Proper accounting and accurate reporting  
- Prompt response to Freddie Mac and Borrowers  
- Adequate staffing and operational capacity  
- Compliance with all applicable laws, Guide provisions, FHA/VA/RHS/MI requirements, and Freddie Mac instructions  

Servicer duties include paying or verifying Borrower payments for:

- Property insurance premiums  
- Mortgage insurance premiums  
- Ground rents  
- Property taxes  
- HOA/Condo/Co-op assessments  
- Utility assessments  
- Charges, fines or impositions related to the property  

The Servicer must defend Freddie Mac’s interest in the Mortgage or REO as it would defend its own.

The Servicer must maintain a **business continuity plan** (Section 1302.3).  
In a disaster, the Servicer must notify Freddie Mac within 24 hours.

---

## (b) Servicer Performs Duties at Own Expense
The Servicer performs all obligations under the Servicing Contract **at its own expense**, unless otherwise stated.  
Any Servicer-incurred costs may be charged to the Borrower only when permitted under:

- The Security Instrument  
- The Servicing Contract  
- Applicable law  

---

## (c) Servicer as Independent Contractor
The Servicer acts as an **independent contractor**, not as an agent of Freddie Mac.

---

## (d) Servicer Indemnification
The Servicer must indemnify Freddie Mac for any loss, damage or expense (including attorney fees and court costs) resulting from the Servicer’s failure to perform required duties.
