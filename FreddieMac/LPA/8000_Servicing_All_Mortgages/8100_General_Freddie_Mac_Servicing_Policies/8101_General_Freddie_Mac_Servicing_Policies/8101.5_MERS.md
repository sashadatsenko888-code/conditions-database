Source: Freddie Mac Seller/Servicer Guide  
Section: 8101.5 – MERS®  
Effective Date: 09/10/2025
---

## Overview
Section 8101.5 outlines Freddie Mac’s requirements for **MERS® registration, notices, reconciliation, signing authority, and deactivation** for Mortgages serviced for Freddie Mac.

---

## (a) Post-Purchase MERS Registration
- When registering a Mortgage in MERS after sale to Freddie Mac, the Servicer must notify MERS that **Freddie Mac is the investor**.  
- The Servicer must provide **Freddie Mac loan number** to MERS.  
- Mortgages secured by **Manufactured Homes in certificate-of-title States** cannot be registered with MERS (see 5703.4(f)(iii)).

---

## (b) MERS Rule 14 Requirements
Servicers handling **Legal Filings** under MERS Rule 14 must submit all required notifications and information to MERS **on Freddie Mac’s behalf**.

---

## (c) MERS Notices of Liens or Legal Actions
- MERS, as **mortgagee of record**, may receive lien/legal action notices.  
- If the notice identifies the Servicer, MERS forwards it directly.  
- If MERS cannot identify the Mortgage/Servicer, it will broadcast an **unidentified notice** to all MERS members.  
- Servicers must:
  - Check MERS messages **daily**  
  - Request more information from MERS if they cannot determine applicability  
  - Act timely on valid notices and inform MERS so records can be updated

---

## (d) System-to-System Reconciliation
Seller/Servicers must comply with **all MERS data reconciliation requirements** defined in the MERS Governing Documents.

---

## (e) MERS Signing Officer Requirements
Only individuals **authorized by MERS corporate resolution** may act as MERS signing officers for the Seller/Servicer.

---

## (f) Deactivation from MERS
- A Servicer may deactivate a Mortgage from MERS at its discretion.  
- Upon deactivation, the Servicer must:
  - Prepare a recordable assignment from **MERS → Servicer**  
  - Record it where required by law  
- MERS will notify Freddie Mac of **any deactivation** involving a Mortgage in which Freddie Mac has an interest.
