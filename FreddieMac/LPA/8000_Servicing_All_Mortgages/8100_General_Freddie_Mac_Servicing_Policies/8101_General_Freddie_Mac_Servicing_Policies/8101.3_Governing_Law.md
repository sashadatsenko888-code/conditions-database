## Overview

Section 8101.3 establishes the governing law that applies to all Purchase Documents or other contracts for the Servicing of Mortgages.

---

## Governing Law Requirements

Each Purchase Document or contract related to Servicing must be interpreted, and the rights and obligations of the parties determined, according to **the laws of the United States**.

If no applicable federal precedent exists, and applying state law does not conflict with or frustrate any provision of the Purchase Document, **the laws of the State of New York** are deemed to reflect the laws of the United States.
