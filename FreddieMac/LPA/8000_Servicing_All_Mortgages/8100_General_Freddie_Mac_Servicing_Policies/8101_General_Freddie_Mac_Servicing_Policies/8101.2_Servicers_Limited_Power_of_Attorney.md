Section: 8101.2 – Servicer’s Limited Power of Attorney; Execution of Documents  
Effective Date: 09/10/2025  

---

## Overview
Section 8101.2 describes the Servicer’s authority under Freddie Mac’s **limited power of attorney**, obligations for recording and handling documents, and rules for **execution of documents** on Freddie Mac’s behalf.

---

## (a) Servicer’s Limited Power of Attorney

The Servicer must accept Freddie Mac’s limited power of attorney authorizing the Servicer to:

- Perform all mortgagee duties in **canceling the Note and Security Instrument** upon Borrower payoff.  
- Execute, endorse, and acknowledge specific documents in Freddie Mac’s name (as outlined in Exhibit 53).

**Servicer obligations:**

1. Comply with all **local recording requirements**.  
2. Assume all **recording fees** required by local authorities.  
3. Provide Freddie Mac, upon request, with a list of **all jurisdictions** where the POA has been recorded.

A sample limited POA is located in **Exhibit 53 – Limited Power of Attorney**.

---

## (b) Servicer’s Execution of Documents

Requests for Freddie Mac to execute documents must be:

- Submitted directly to Freddie Mac (see Directory 9).  
- Accompanied by the **Request for Assistance Form**  
  (https://sf.freddiemac.com/docs/pdf/fact-sheet/sats_instructions.pdf)

Freddie Mac will return executed documents only after confirming all required conditions are satisfied.

### Servicers **must NOT** submit documents for Freddie Mac’s signature if they are authorized to sign under the POA.

### The Servicer **may execute in Freddie Mac’s name**:

- Release documents  
- Assignments of the Security Instrument for foreclosure initiation  
- Other documents listed in Exhibit 53

A Servicer may request a limited POA via Directory 9.  
Freddie Mac may also require the Servicer to obtain and use the POA for specific transactions.
