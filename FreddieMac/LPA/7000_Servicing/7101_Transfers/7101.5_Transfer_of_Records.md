## Overview
Sets **what records must be transferred, in what format, and by when** during a Transfer of Servicing. Distinguishes between:  
- **(a)** Mortgage/REO file contents (due **≤ 30 days after** Effective Date of Transfer),  
- **(b)** Portfolio-level records (due **no later than** the **Effective Date of Transfer**), and  
- **(c)** **Additional** items for **Subsequent Transfers** (due **≤ 30 days after**).

---

## (a) Transfer of Mortgage & REO files — due ≤ 30 days after Effective Date
**Deliver per loan (and each REO):**
- **Mortgage file** maintained per Ch. 3302 (retain required paper originals per §3302.2).  
  - If storage formats differ between Transferor/Transferee, **convert** to Transferee’s format **or** generate **paper copies**.  
  - For **Notes dated ≥ 07/01/2013**: **No microfilm/microfiche** copies (except the specific paper originals required by §3302.2). Copies must be via scanning systems in commonly used electronic formats: **PDF**, **TIF**, **JPEG**, or other commonly used electronic formats.
- **Payment history**: Full payment & escrow disbursement history (incl. **latest escrow analysis**) with support, **from Origination Date**.
- **Correspondence & reports**: All borrower communications and, as applicable, FHA/VA/RHS/MI/Freddie Mac/governmental correspondence.
- **Notice of transfer**: Copy of borrower notice(s) re Transfer of Servicing.
- **REO history** (if applicable): Receipts, expenses, management/marketing activity, copies of any filed MI claims, **from REO acquisition date**.

---

## (b) Transfer of portfolio records — due by the Effective Date
**Deliver at portfolio level (covering all Mortgages/REO in the transfer):**
- **Notices to third parties** per §7101.7(b), incl. **MI approval/commitment** documentation.
- **Service contracts**: Copies of tax service and flood hazard determination contracts (if applicable).
- **Unpaid charges**: List of escrowed amounts due and unpaid **as of Effective Date**.
- **Trial balances** (as of close of last Business Day immediately before Effective Date), showing:
  - Transfers of Ownership, payoffs, other Servicing exceptions in process  
  - Escrows, escrow advances, prepayments  
  - Where applicable: buydown accounts/balances  
  - Delinquencies, foreclosures, bankruptcies, REO
- **Automatic payments**: List of loans on auto-draft.
- **Insurance policy tracking**: List of loans with property insurance **expiration dates** and whether premiums are escrowed.
- **Fair lending data elements** per §1301.2(f) for **each Mortgage**.
- **Other documents**: Ledger records **and** code definitions used in ledgers, trial balances, or any other docs Freddie Mac requires to transfer.

---

## (c) Additional requirements — Subsequent Transfers (due ≤ 30 days after Effective Date)
- **Custodial Accounts**: Copy of depository **reconciliation** as of the bank’s last business day **immediately preceding** the Effective Date for **each** Custodial Account (Ch. 8304).
- **Freddie Mac reports**: Copies of all **Servicing & accounting reports** filed with Freddie Mac for the **three months immediately preceding** the Effective Date.
