Section: 7101.6 – Transfer of Funds in Transfers of Servicing  
Effective Date: 09/10/2025  

> **Condition-Relevant Section — Include in Ruleset**

---

## Overview
Defines how **funds and related correspondence** must be transferred when Servicing moves from a **Transferor Servicer** to a **Transferee Servicer**. Ensures **custodial integrity**, accurate **reconciliation**, and **timely reimbursement** of any advances as of the **Effective Date of Transfer**.

---

## (a) Transfer of Funds

### (i) General Requirement
- The **Transferor Servicer** must transfer **all account balances** (Escrows, prepayments, buydown funds, etc.) to the **Transferee Servicer’s depository** in a manner ensuring:
  - Funds remain **at all times held in custodial capacity** for Freddie Mac (§8302.1(c)).
  - A **final reconciliation** of all related monies is completed **on the Effective Date of Transfer**.

### (ii) Escrow Accounts
- The Transferor Servicer must forward the **entire balance of all Custodial Accounts** — including:
  - Escrows,  
  - Buydown funds,  
  - Repair accounts, and  
  - Replacement reserves —  
  **net of documented advances**,  
  to the Transferee Servicer’s depository **on the Effective Date of Transfer**.

### (iii) Delinquent Interest
- Any **delinquent interest** previously **advanced to Freddie Mac** by the Transferor Servicer as of the Effective Date (minus any prepaid interest) must be:
  - **Reimbursed** to the Transferor Servicer by the Transferee Servicer,  
  - **No later than** the date the funds are due to Freddie Mac.

### (iv) Delinquent Principal
- Any **delinquent principal** previously **advanced to Freddie Mac** by the Transferor Servicer as of the Effective Date (minus any prepaid principal) must be:
  - **Reimbursed** to the Transferor Servicer by the Transferee Servicer,  
  - **No later than** the **Effective Date of Transfer**.

---

## (b) Funds & Correspondence Received After Transfer
- If the Transferor Servicer receives **any funds or correspondence** related to transferred Mortgages or REO **on or after** the Effective Date of Transfer:
  - It must **forward them to the Transferee Servicer** **within one Business Day** of receipt.
