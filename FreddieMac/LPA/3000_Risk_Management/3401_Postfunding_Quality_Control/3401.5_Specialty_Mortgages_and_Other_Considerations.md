## Overview

Section 3401.5 outlines documentation requirements for several specialty mortgage types that require additional evidence in the postfunding QC process:

- Mortgages secured by Manufactured Homes  
- Community Land Trust Mortgages  
- GreenCHOICE Mortgages®

Each category contains unique documentation obligations related to property classification, titling, approvals, energy-efficiency improvements, and escrow/installation requirements.

---

## (a) Mortgages Secured by Manufactured Homes

The Mortgage file must include **evidence that the Manufactured Home is legally recognized as real property**, including:

- Documentation proving the Manufactured Home is **legally classified as real property**  
- Evidence the home is **properly titled**  
- Evidence the lien is **properly created, evidenced, and perfected**

For **new Manufactured Homes**, the Mortgage file must additionally include:

- The **manufacturer’s invoice**  
- The **Manufactured Home Purchase Agreement**

For **new installations**, the file must include:

- Evidence the unit was installed **in compliance with applicable HUD Manufactured Home Codes**

---

## (b) Community Land Trust Mortgages (CLT)

The Mortgage file must include:

- **If applicable**, written approval signed by the Community Land Trust (or its authorized representative) permitting the Borrower to **refinance**
- An **original executed or certified copy** of:
  - The **Community Land Trust ground lease**  
  - **Form 490 – Community Land Trust Ground Lease Rider**, including recordation information

---

## (c) GreenCHOICE Mortgages®

For GreenCHOICE Mortgages, the Mortgage file must include:

1. **Documentation of eligible improvements**  
   - As described in **Section 4606.1(b)**  
   - Including receipts, invoices, contractor statements, etc.

2. **Documentation of costs** used to calculate the **total acquisition cost** for purchase transactions  
   - As referenced in **Section 4606.2(a)**

3. For **Manually Underwritten Mortgages** using one or both higher qualifying ratios:  
   - Evidence the property has **greater energy efficiency** than a “standard” (non-energy-efficient) property  
   - As required under **Section 5401.1(e)(ii)(B)**

4. For **improvements completed after the Note Date**:  
   - The **written escrow agreement**  
   - As required under **Section 4606.3(a)(3)**

5. **If applicable:**  
   - An **energy report** or alternative approved documentation  
   - A **completion report** by the appraiser  
   - Documentation meeting **Section 4606.3(b)** when proceeds were used to pay **Existing Debt** (as defined in 4606.1(c))
