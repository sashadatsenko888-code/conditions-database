## Overview

Section 3401.3 specifies the **mandatory documentation** that must be included in every Mortgage file submitted for postfunding quality control (QC) review.  
These requirements cover:

- Credit documentation  
- Employment and income verification  
- Mortgage payment history  
- Verification of funds  
- Verification of prior housing payment history (for Manual Underwriting)

All documents must be present, accurate, and sufficient to allow Freddie Mac to confirm borrower capacity, creditworthiness, and payment history.

---

## (a) Credit Documentation

The Mortgage file must include:

- A **credit report** for the Borrower(s)  
- All **Credit Scores** used in underwriting  
- Documentation showing:
  - The **selected Credit Score** for each Borrower  
  - The **method/process used to select the score**  
  - This information must appear on **Form 1077 (Uniform Underwriting and Transmittal Summary)** or a similar document

The file must also contain **all source documentation** used to obtain or verify the Credit Score(s).

---

## (b) Verification of Employment and Income

The Mortgage file must include:

- Documentation used to **verify employment**  
- Documentation used to **verify income**  
- A **written analysis** of the income used to qualify the Borrower  
  - Analysis must reflect stable income, allowable income types, and qualification calculations

---

## (c) Mortgage Payment History

The Mortgage file must include a complete payment history covering:

- The **first payment Due Date** → **Delivery Date**

The payment history must:

- Show **no 30-day delinquencies or more**  
- Be **readable without code translation** (plain-text or clearly labeled)

---

## (d) Verification of Source of Funds

The Mortgage file must include documentation verifying **all eligible sources of funds** used for:

- Down payment  
- Closing costs  
- Reserves  
- Any other required funds for qualification

---

## (e) Verification of Previous Mortgage/Rental Payment History

For **Manually Underwritten Mortgages**, the Mortgage file must contain:

- Evidence of Mortgage payment history **and/or rental payment history**  
- Covering the **12 months prior** to the Mortgage application date  
- Demonstrating timely payments and no material delinquencies
