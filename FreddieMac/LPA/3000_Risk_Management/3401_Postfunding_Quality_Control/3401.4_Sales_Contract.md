## Overview

Section 3401.4 specifies the documentation requirements for purchase transactions in the postfunding QC review.  
The Seller must maintain a fully executed sales contract (including all addenda) and the final settlement/closing disclosure with supporting cost documentation.

These documents validate the accuracy of the purchase transaction, settlement costs, interested party contributions, and compliance with Freddie Mac requirements.

---

## (a) Sales Contract

For **purchase Mortgages**, the Mortgage file must include:

- A **fully executed sales contract**, signed by all parties, and  
- **All addenda**, amendments, revisions, counteroffers, and related documentation.

The contract must clearly identify:

- Purchase price  
- Property address  
- Parties to the transaction  
- Any Seller concessions or negotiated terms  

---

## (b) Settlement/Closing Disclosure Statement

The Mortgage file must include:

- The **final Settlement Statement** or **Closing Disclosure (CD)**  
- All related documentation that shows **every cost** to:
  - The **homebuyer**, and  
  - The **property seller**, when applicable

Supporting documentation includes but is not limited to:

- Addenda to the CD  
- Seller-side closing statements  
- Final invoices and fee disclosures  
- Proof of prepaid items, escrows, and adjustments
