Section: 3402.5 – Reverifications Made by Seller

## Overview

Section 3402.5 defines the reverification requirements for all Loan Product Advisor® and Non-LPA Mortgages selected for **post-closing quality control**.  
Reverifications confirm the validity and accuracy of original underwriting information and protect against fraud, misrepresentation, and erroneous documentation.

Reverifications:

- Must begin **as soon as possible after sample selection**  
- May be **written or verbal**  
- Must be **retained in the Mortgage file**

---

# (a) Verifications of Employment, Income, and Sources of Funds

## (i) Required Reverifications

Except where exceptions apply, the Seller must:

- Reverify **all employment**, **all income**, and **all sources of funds** used in underwriting  
- Use the **minimum required documentation** from the original process  
- Send copies of original verifications to issuers requesting confirmation  
- Pay all fees charged for obtaining reverification information  

### ✔ Exceptions (No Reverification Required)

Exceptions apply **only if no evidence of misrepresentation** exists in the file.

| Documentation Type | Reverification Requirement |
|--------------------|----------------------------|
| Income, employment, or asset information obtained from a **Freddie-designated third-party service provider** or **Freddie Mac financial institution verification report** | **Not required**, if accessed directly from verified databases or IRS transcripts (Sections 5303.4(b), 5303.5(b), 5305.2(b), 5501.8(b), 5304.2(e)(iii)) |
| 10-day pre-closing verification (10-day PCV) confirmed by LPA Feedback Certificate or verification report | **Not required** if Note Date ≤ “close-by” date on Feedback Certificate. See Section 5302.6. |

Freddie-designated AIM service providers list:  
https://sf.freddiemac.com/tools-learning/loan-advisor/our-solutions/aim-asset-income-modeler

## Required Formats for Reverifications

### **Verbal Reverifications**

Documentation must include:

- Name of QC reviewer  
- Name of employer/bank/gift donor, etc.  
- Name & title of the contact  
- Date(s) of contact  
- Confirmation of accuracy (or identification of inaccuracies)  
- Phone number obtained **only from independent third-party sources**  
- Name of source (directory, reputable website, 411, etc.)

### **Email Reverifications**

Must be an email exchange from the employer's **independently obtained work email**, including:

- Borrower name  
- Employer name  
- Name, title, work email of employer contact  
- Date of contact  
- Borrower’s current employment status  

Mortgage file must include:

- Documentation of the independent source used to obtain employer email  
- Name, title, and employer of the Seller’s representative obtaining the reverification

Freddie Mac may **require additional reverifications** at its discretion and may independently reverify any information.

---

## (ii) Tax Information Requirements

Except where noted:

- Seller must obtain **IRS tax information** using **Form 4506-C** or a permitted alternative (e.g., IRS Form 8821)

### ✔ Exceptions (No Tax Transcript Required)

| Documentation Type | Requirement |
|--------------------|-------------|
| All qualifying income obtained via designated third-party provider or Freddie Mac FI verification report | Not required for eligible income types (5303.4(b), 5303.5(b), 5305.2(b)) |
| IRS tax information already obtained during origination | Not required |

### Special Rules for U.S. Territories

- **Puerto Rico** → Must use PR **Form 2907**  
- **Guam / U.S. Virgin Islands** → Must submit Form 4506-C or equivalent to local tax authorities

---

# (b) Borrower’s Social Security Number Validation

Seller must validate SSNs for all post-closing QC sample loans unless:

- SSN was validated during **origination** or **pre-closing QC**, **and**  
- Validation was **not** performed by a Mortgage Broker/Correspondent, **and**  
- No misrepresentation is indicated in file

---

# (c) Credit Reports

### Loan Product Advisor® Mortgages

- No new credit report required  
- Seller must verify accuracy of:
  - Borrower identity (name, address history, SSN)  
  - LPA inputs made on/before Note Date  
- Must review any **non-LPA credit information** obtained externally

### LPA Accept Mortgages

- Seller does **not** need to assess whether LPA underwrote the credit properly  
- Seller **must** verify credit reports belong to the correct Borrower

### Non-LPA Mortgages

- **1 of every 10** → must obtain:
  - New RMCR or  
  - 3-repository merged in-file  
- Remaining loans → must obtain at least one new in-file report from one or more repositories  

### Manually Underwritten Mortgages

- Seller must **re-underwrite** the credit  
- Must comply with Section 3402.7

### All Mortgages

- Seller must determine whether additional credit was opened due to **recent inquiries (past 90 days)**

---

# (d) Verification of Owner-Occupancy

For all QC-selected mortgages secured by **Primary Residences**, Seller must:

- Verify the Borrower **occupies the property** as their Primary Residence  

---

# (e) Property Valuation Requirements

Seller must ensure the appraisal or valuation meets Freddie Mac requirements.  
QC review may include: desk reviews, field reviews, AVMs, MLS data, public records, online tools.

Enhanced Relief Refinance® mortgages may be excluded.

### Reporting Requirement

If a QC finding affects eligibility:

- Report to Freddie Mac **within 90 days**, or  
- **Within 60 days** if fraud/suspected fraud (per Section 3402.10)

---

## (i) Mortgages Originated With Appraisals

### **(A) No Value Representation & Warranty Relief**

Seller must obtain a **desk review** assessing:

- Data accuracy  
- Comparable sale appropriateness  
- Rationale supporting final value  
- Property eligibility  

If desk review insufficient → must obtain a **field review**:

- 1-unit → Form **1032**  
- 2–4 units → Form **1072**  

If an adequate field review was done earlier → no new one required.

---

### **(B) Appraised Value Rep & Warranty Relief (LCA score ≤ 2.5)**

Seller must:

- Ensure compliance with Section 5602.2  
- Perform all parts of (A) **except**:
  - No need to assess appraiser’s opinion of value  
  - No need to analyze comparable sales  

Focus is on:

- Data quality  
- Property condition  
- Marketability  
- Collateral adequacy  

---

## (ii) Mortgages Originated with ACE Waivers (ACE or ACE+ PDR)

If originated with:

- **ACE appraisal waiver (5602.3)**  
- **ACE+ PDR (5602.4)**  

Seller does **not** need to comply with (i)(A) or (i)(B).  
Seller must confirm:

- Data submitted to LPA was accurate  
- Eligibility requirements of 5602.3 or 5602.4 were met
