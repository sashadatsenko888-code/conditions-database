Section: 3603.2 – Servicing Transfers and Compensation  
Effective Date: 11/19/2025  

> **Condition-Relevant Section – Include in Ruleset**  

---

## ■ Overview  
Section 3603.2 outlines Freddie Mac’s requirements for:  
- Transfers of the Servicing Contract and Servicing Contract Rights  
- Servicer compensation during a Transfer  
- Treatment of outstanding purchase commitments when a Servicer is terminated  

---

## ■ (a) Transfer of Servicing Contract and Related Servicing Contract Rights  

If Freddie Mac transfers all or part of the Servicing Contract and related Servicing Contract Rights for any Mortgages:

- **Servicing compensation** (per Section 8105.1(a)) is paid **to the Transferee Servicer**  
- Except where otherwise stated in applicable Purchase Documents with that Transferee Servicer  
- Freddie Mac retains the right to **negotiate the Servicing fee** with the subsequent Servicer  

Freddie Mac has the **exclusive right** to receive any amount paid by the Transferee Servicer **for the right to the Servicing compensation** under the Servicing Contract.

---

## ■ (b) Outstanding Purchase Commitments  

If a terminated Servicer has outstanding commitments for Freddie Mac to purchase additional Mortgages, Freddie Mac may, at its discretion:

1. **Transfer Servicing** of those Mortgages to a Transferee Servicer immediately upon funding  
2. **Assign** the outstanding commitments to another Seller  
3. **Pair off** the outstanding commitments  
4. **Cancel** the outstanding commitments
