Section: 3603.3 – Documentation and Remittance Requirements  
Effective Date: 11/19/2025

## Overview
Section 3603.3 outlines the **documentation, records, and remittance obligations** when Freddie Mac terminates the Servicing Contract and related Servicing Contract Rights for any Mortgage.  
It specifies the Servicer’s responsibilities for supplying all required documents, transferring escrow funds, and ensuring an orderly Transfer of Servicing.

---

## (a) Documents and Records

Upon termination of Servicing Contract and related Servicing Contract Rights, the Servicer must:

- Supply **all reports, documents, and information** requested by Freddie Mac.  
- Ensure the **Document Custodian** also supplies required materials (if applicable).  
- Include **all Mortgage records required under Section 8101.4(c)**.  
- Prepare all materials in the **exact form requested by Freddie Mac**.  
- Deliver all documentation to:
  - The **Transferee Servicer**, and  
  - The **new Document Custodian** (if Freddie Mac assigns one).

The documentation must include:

- **Current accounting/status** of each Mortgage covered by the termination  
- All supporting file components requested by Freddie Mac  

---

## (b) Remittance to Freddie Mac

For every Mortgage whose Servicing Contract and related Servicing Contract Rights are terminated, the Servicer must:

- Remit **all Mortgage collections** on the date specified by Freddie Mac  
  (even if this date differs from regular remittance requirements)

- Transfer **all Escrow Funds**, including:
  - Escrow account balances  
  - Escrow sub-accounts  
  - Prepaid installments  

- Ensure all funds and records are transferred to the **Transferee Servicer** on Freddie Mac’s specified date.

The Servicer must use **best efforts** to ensure an orderly, complete, and timely Transfer of Servicing.
