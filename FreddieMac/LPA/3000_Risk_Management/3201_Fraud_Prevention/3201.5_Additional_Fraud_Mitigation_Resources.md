## Overview
This section provides information about **additional fraud prevention and detection resources** available to Seller/Servicers through Freddie Mac.  
These resources are intended to assist in strengthening internal controls, improving fraud awareness, and supporting investigations of suspicious mortgage-related activity.

---

## Available Resources

1. **Freddie Mac Fraud Prevention Website:**  
   Seller/Servicers can access Freddie Mac’s fraud prevention and detection resources online at:  
   [https://sf.freddiemac.com/working-with-us/fraud-prevention/overview](https://sf.freddiemac.com/working-with-us/fraud-prevention/overview)

   This resource includes:
   - Fraud prevention guidelines and checklists  
   - Emerging fraud trends and red flags  
   - Best practices for investigation and reporting  

2. **Freddie Mac Fraud Hotline:**  
   Additional guidance or information can be obtained through the **Freddie Mac Fraud Hotline** at:  
   **800-4FRAUD8 (800-437-2838)**

   The hotline provides Seller/Servicers with a direct point of contact for:
   - Reporting fraud-related questions or concerns  
   - Requesting clarification about Freddie Mac’s fraud policies  
   - Receiving general guidance on fraud prevention procedures  

3. **Freddie Mac Exclusionary List:**  
   Seller/Servicers can also review the **Freddie Mac Exclusionary List**, available through **Freddie Mac Gateway**, to verify that parties involved in origination or Servicing are not prohibited from participation.
