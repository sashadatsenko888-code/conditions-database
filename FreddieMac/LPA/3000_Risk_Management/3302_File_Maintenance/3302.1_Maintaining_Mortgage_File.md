## Overview
This section sets forth Freddie Mac’s requirements for **maintaining individual Mortgage files** for all Mortgages serviced on Freddie Mac’s behalf.  
It ensures that each Mortgage file contains all necessary documentation, servicing records, and collection history to support transparency, compliance, and auditability.

---

## Requirements

1. **Individual Mortgage Files:**  
   - The Servicer must maintain a **separate, complete file** for each Mortgage serviced for Freddie Mac.  
   - Each file must include:
     - All documents required to be held by the Seller under **Chapter 3301**, and  
     - All additional materials related to the Mortgage, including:
       - Legal notices,  
       - Correspondence,  
       - Forms, and  
       - Reports concerning the Mortgage, the Mortgaged Premises, or any related **Real Estate Owned (REO)**.

2. **Collection Records:**  
   - The Servicer must maintain **adequate and detailed records of collection efforts** for each Mortgage.  
   - These records must be **available to Freddie Mac upon request** and, at minimum, include:
     - Dates when **letters and notices** were mailed to the Borrower,  
     - Dates and **results of all collection activities**,  
     - Documented **reasons for current and prior defaults**,  
     - **Terms and details** of any arrangements made to cure a delinquency or provide an alternative to foreclosure, and  
     - **Documentation of property inspections**, as required by **Section 9202.3(b)**.

3. **Recordkeeping Standards:**  
   - Mortgage files may be maintained **in paper or electronic form**, provided they are complete, secure, and easily retrievable.  
   - Servicers must ensure proper version control, protection of borrower information, and timely updates to all servicing records.

---

### Minimum File Contents Summary

| File Component | Required Contents |
|----------------|-------------------|
| Core Documentation | All documents required by Chapter 3301 |
| Legal & Administrative | Notices, correspondence, reports |
| Collection History | Dates, results, borrower contact logs |
| Default & Loss Mitigation | Reasons for default, repayment terms, modification agreements |
| Property Inspections | Evidence per Section 9202.3(b) |
