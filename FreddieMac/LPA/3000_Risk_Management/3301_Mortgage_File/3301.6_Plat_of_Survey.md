---

## Overview
This section defines Freddie Mac’s requirement for including a **plat of survey** in the Mortgage file.  
A plat of survey provides a detailed map of the property boundaries and is required only when specified by Freddie Mac’s underwriting or collateral documentation standards.

---

## Requirements

1. **Inclusion in Mortgage File:**  
   - The Mortgage file must contain a **plat of survey** when required under **Section 4702.2(e)**.  
   - This document verifies property boundaries, easements, encroachments, and improvements, as applicable.

2. **Reference to Section 4702.2(e):**  
   - Section 4702.2(e) outlines when a plat of survey is necessary—typically based on property type, jurisdictional requirements, or lender-specific appraisal and title standards.

3. **Format and Retention:**  
   - The plat may be retained in **paper or electronic form**, consistent with Freddie Mac’s recordkeeping requirements.  
   - It must be clearly associated with the applicable Mortgage and retrievable upon Freddie Mac’s request.
