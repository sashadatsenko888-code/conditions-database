## Overview
This section defines Freddie Mac’s documentation requirements for **mortgage insurance policies** in the Mortgage file.  
It ensures that when a Mortgage requires mortgage insurance (MI), proper evidence of coverage is maintained and verifiable within the Seller/Servicer’s records.

---

## Requirements

1. **Applicability:**  
   - This requirement applies to all **Mortgages required to carry mortgage insurance**, including those insured by private mortgage insurance (PMI) providers approved by Freddie Mac.

2. **Required Documentation:**  
   - The Mortgage file must include **one of the following**:
     - The **actual mortgage insurance policy**, **or**  
     - **Proof of insurance** issued by the insurer, showing:
       - The insured property address,  
       - The Borrower’s name,  
       - The MI coverage amount or percentage,  
       - The effective date of coverage, and  
       - The mortgage insurance company’s name and policy number.

3. **Format and Retention:**  
   - Documentation may be **paper or electronic**, provided it clearly evidences MI coverage and is **retrievable upon request** by Freddie Mac.  
   - Servicers must maintain the policy or proof of insurance **for the life of the Mortgage** or as required under Freddie Mac’s record retention policies.

4. **Validation:**  
   - The Seller/Servicer must ensure that:
     - The insurance coverage complies with Freddie Mac’s MI eligibility requirements.  
     - The insurer is **approved and in good standing** with Freddie Mac.  
     - The MI coverage amount satisfies Freddie Mac’s LTV-based requirements.
