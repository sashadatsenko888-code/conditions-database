## Overview
This section establishes Freddie Mac’s requirements for **maintaining, retaining, and transferring Mortgage files**.  
It defines file ownership, documentation content, and data retention standards for Sellers and Servicers, including those operating under Servicing-Released Sales processes.

---

## Requirements

1. **File Maintenance:**  
   - The **Seller (if servicing for Freddie Mac)** and any **Transferee Servicer** must maintain the Mortgage file in accordance with **Chapter 3302**.  
   - Each Servicer must maintain the Mortgage file **for as long as it services the Mortgage**.  
   - The file must include all applicable documents listed in **Chapter 3401** and this chapter.

2. **Servicing-Released Sales:**  
   - Sellers that sell Mortgages under the **Servicing-Released Sales Process** must comply with all file and data retention requirements defined in **Exhibit 28A, Loan Servicing Purchase and Sale Agreement for Cash-Released XChange®**.

3. **Transferor Servicer Copies:**  
   - Except for Servicing-Released Sales, Transferor Servicers may retain copies of the Mortgage file to facilitate Servicing transfers.  
   - Sellers and Transferor Servicers retaining copies must do so until the later of:
     - **Seven years** after all representation and warranty obligations expire (per **Loan Coverage Advisor®**), or  
     - After any **Transfer of Servicing**.

4. **File Ownership:**  
   - The Mortgage file, its contents, and all copies (including electronic data) are the **property of Freddie Mac** at all times.  
   - Refer to **Section 1201.9** for detailed ownership and data rights provisions.

5. **Social Security Number Handling:**  
   Sellers may redact or delete the Borrower’s SSN from physical documents **only if all of the following are met**:
   - The complete SSN is delivered to Freddie Mac as required in **Section 6302.9**.  
   - Unredacted documents are provided when the file is submitted for **quality control review (Section 3401.1)**.  
   - The SSN is maintained electronically as part of the Mortgage file.  
   - The SSN is provided to the **Servicing Transferee** during any Servicing transfer.
