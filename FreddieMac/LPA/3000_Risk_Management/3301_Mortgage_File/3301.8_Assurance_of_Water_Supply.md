Section: 3301.8 – Assurance of Water Supply  
Effective Date: 03/02/2016  

> **Condition-Relevant Section — Include in Ruleset**

---

## Overview
This section establishes Freddie Mac’s documentation requirements for **properties dependent on private or shareholder-based water or irrigation companies**.  
When a property’s water supply depends on ownership in such a company, Freddie Mac requires evidence that the property owner is entitled to a sufficient and transferable water supply.

---

## Requirements

1. **Applicability:**  
   - This requirement applies when the **Mortgaged Premises** depend on a **water or irrigation company** that supplies water **exclusively to its shareholders**.

2. **Required Documentation:**  
   - The Mortgage file must include a **stock certificate** that:
     - Is **duly endorsed to Freddie Mac**, and  
     - **Entitles the property owner** to an adequate and ongoing supply of water for the Mortgaged Premises.

3. **Endorsement and Validity:**  
   - The stock certificate must:
     - Be **legally valid** and current at the time of sale to Freddie Mac.  
     - Clearly identify the shareholder (property owner) and property address.  
     - Be endorsed in compliance with applicable state transfer laws.

4. **Retention:**  
   - The endorsed certificate must be retained in the Mortgage file (paper or electronic) and available for Freddie Mac’s review upon request.  
   - The Servicer must ensure the document remains traceable in the event of any Servicing transfer.
