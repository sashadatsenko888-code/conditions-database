## Overview
This section details Freddie Mac’s documentation requirements for **Mortgages secured by property in New York State** that are documented using a **New York Consolidation, Extension, and Modification Agreement (NY CEMA)**.  
It specifies which documents must be included in the Mortgage file, based on the NY CEMA form version and the status of prior consolidations.

---

## Requirements

### (a) General Requirements for All NY CEMA Mortgages
For all Mortgages documented using an NY CEMA, the Mortgage file must include:

1. **Consolidated Note:**  
   A copy of the **Consolidated Note** showing **all endorsements**.

2. **Old and New Money Notes:**  
   Unless Original Notes are required under **Section 3401.3**, include copies of:
   - The **Original Old Money Note**, and  
   - Any **Original New Money (Gap) Notes**.

3. **Original NY CEMA Document:**  
   - The **Original NY CEMA** with official recordation notation.  
   - If the recorder retains the original document, a **certified copy** issued by the recorder is acceptable.

---

### (b) Additional Requirements for NY CEMA Form Versions

#### 1. **Form 3172, 1/01 or Form 3172, 1/01 (rev. 5/01):**
If the most recent prior Mortgage was originated using **Form 3172, 1/01** or **(rev. 5/01):**

The Mortgage file must include:
- The **prior NY CEMA** with all exhibits attached,  
- The **Original Consolidated Note** from the prior consolidation,  
- The **Original Old Money Note**, and  
- The **Original New Money (Gap) Note**, if applicable.

If **new funds** were advanced during the current consolidation, the corresponding **Original Old Money Note** and **Original New Money (Gap) Note** must be:
- **Originals signed by the Borrower**, and  
- **Endorsed in blank**.

---

#### 2. **Form 3172, 7/86:**
If the prior Mortgage was consolidated under **NY CEMA Form 3172, 7/86**, the Mortgage file must include:
- The **prior NY CEMA** with all exhibits,  
- The **Original Old Money Note**,  
- The **Original New Money (Gap) Note**, and  
- For any new funds advanced, the **Original Notes** must again be signed and endorsed in blank.

---

#### 3. **Non-Consolidated Prior Mortgages:**
If the most recent prior Mortgage was **not** originated using NY CEMA Forms 3172 (any version), the Mortgage file must contain:
- The **Original Old Money Note**,  
- The **Original New Money (Gap) Note**, and  
- Any newly advanced funds represented by **Original Notes**, signed and endorsed in blank.

---

### Documentation Summary

| Scenario | Required Documents |
|-----------|--------------------|
| All NY CEMA Mortgages | Consolidated Note (endorsed), Old Money Note, New Money Note, Original NY CEMA with recordation |
| Form 3172, 1/01 or rev. 5/01 | Prior NY CEMA + all exhibits, Consolidated Note, Old and New Money Notes (endorsed) |
| Form 3172, 7/86 | Prior NY CEMA + exhibits, Old and New Money Notes (endorsed) |
| Non-CEMA Prior Mortgage | Original Old and New Money Notes (endorsed) |
